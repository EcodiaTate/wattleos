# Your Dashboard

The Dashboard is the first page you see after signing in. It provides a personalised overview based on your role and permissions, with quick actions to jump into your most common tasks.

## Welcome Header

The top of the Dashboard shows a time-aware greeting (Good morning, Good afternoon, or Good evening) with your first name, your school's name, and your current role. This confirms you are signed in to the correct school with the correct account.

## Quick Actions

Below the greeting, you see Quick Action cards - large, clickable tiles that link to your most-used features. Which cards appear depends on your permissions:

- **New Observation** - appears if you have the Create Observation permission. Links directly to the observation capture form.
- **Today's Roll** - appears if you have the Manage Attendance permission. Links to the attendance roll call page with today's date pre-selected.
- **Students** - appears if you have the View Students permission. Links to the student directory.
- **Curriculum** - appears if you have the Manage Curriculum permission. Links to the curriculum browser.
- **Mastery Tracking** - appears if you have the Manage Mastery permission. Links to the mastery grid.
- **Reports** - appears if you have the Manage Reports permission. Links to the reports section.

If you are a parent, you will not see these staff-focused quick actions. Instead, your Dashboard shows your children's information directly.

## Today at a Glance

The "Today at a Glance" section shows cards for the key modules available to you, each linking to the relevant section. The current date is displayed in your school's timezone (set by your administrator). These cards serve as an at-a-glance status for the modules most relevant to your daily work.

## Parent Dashboard

If you are a parent, the Dashboard shows your children's profiles with quick links to view their portfolio, attendance, and reports. If you have multiple children at the school, each child appears as a separate card. Tap a child to see their published observations, mastery progress, and latest reports.

## What the Dashboard Does Not Show (Yet)

The Dashboard currently provides navigation quick actions and contextual links rather than live statistics. Features like today's attendance percentage, number of observations created this week, and pending report counts are planned for future updates. For now, the Dashboard is your launch pad into the rest of WattleOS.
