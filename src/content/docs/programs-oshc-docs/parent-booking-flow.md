# Parent Booking Flow

Parents can browse, book, and manage program sessions for their children directly through the parent portal. This covers everything from signing up for recurring after-school care to booking a single vacation care day, viewing upcoming sessions, and cancelling bookings when plans change.

## Browsing Available Programs

From the parent portal, navigate to **Programs** to see all active programs your school offers. Each program card shows the program name, type, description, schedule (which days it runs and the times), the session fee, and whether spots are available. Programs that your child is not eligible for - based on age restrictions or class eligibility rules - may be filtered or clearly indicated.

Parents can only see programs that the school has marked as active. Inactive or archived programs do not appear in the parent browsing view.

## Booking a Session

Select a program to see its available sessions. Sessions are displayed for the coming weeks, showing the date, time, available spots, and whether the session is open for booking or at capacity.

To book a casual (one-off) session, select the date and confirm. If the session has available capacity, the booking is confirmed immediately and the child's name appears on the session roster. If the session is full, the booking is placed on the waitlist and you will see your waitlist position. When a spot opens up - because another family cancels - WattleOS automatically promotes the next waitlisted child to confirmed status.

The fee displayed at booking time is the amount that will be charged. Casual bookings use the program's casual fee rate if one is set, otherwise the standard session fee. The fee is locked in when you book, so price changes made by the school after your booking do not affect what you are charged.

## Setting Up Recurring Bookings

For families with a regular schedule, recurring booking patterns are more convenient than booking each session individually. When setting up a recurring pattern, you select which days of the week your child should attend (for example, every Tuesday and Thursday) and a start date. You can optionally set an end date - if you leave it open, the pattern continues indefinitely.

Once a pattern is active, WattleOS automatically creates bookings for your child on the matching sessions as they are generated. You do not need to return each week to book - the system handles it. If a session is at capacity when the recurring booking is applied, that specific booking goes to the waitlist while your pattern remains active for future sessions.

You can view your active recurring patterns in the **My Bookings** section, which shows each pattern's program, days, and effective dates. Patterns can be paused (temporarily stopping new bookings from being generated while keeping the pattern saved) or cancelled entirely.

When you cancel a recurring pattern, you have the choice of keeping existing future bookings or cancelling them all at once. Keeping them is useful if you want to stop the recurring schedule but still attend sessions you have already committed to. Cancelling them all is appropriate when your child is leaving the program.

## Viewing Your Bookings

The **My Bookings** page in the parent portal shows all upcoming sessions your child is booked into across all programs. Each booking shows the program name, session date and time, booking type (recurring or casual), and status (confirmed or waitlisted). Past sessions with check-in and check-out times are also visible for your records.

If your child is enrolled in multiple programs - for example, before-school care and a Friday art class - all bookings appear together in a unified view.

## Cancelling a Booking

To cancel a booking, open it from the My Bookings page and confirm the cancellation. WattleOS checks the program's cancellation policy automatically. If you are cancelling within the required notice window (for example, less than 24 hours before the session), the cancellation is flagged as late and the late cancellation fee may apply. If you cancel with sufficient notice, no additional fee is charged.

After your cancellation, if the session had a waitlist, the next child in the queue is automatically promoted to a confirmed booking.

## How Fees Appear on Your Invoice

Program session fees are tracked on each booking with a billing status. When the school processes billing, your session fees are added to your regular invoice. You will see individual line items for each session your child attended (for per-session billing programs) or a single charge for term or annual billing programs. If a fee is waived by the school - for example, as a goodwill credit for a disrupted session - it appears as waived rather than charged.

## Permissions

Parents can create, view, and cancel bookings for their own children. This access is enforced at the database level through guardian relationships - you can only interact with bookings for children where you are a linked guardian. You do not need any special permission beyond being a registered parent with an active guardian link to the child.
