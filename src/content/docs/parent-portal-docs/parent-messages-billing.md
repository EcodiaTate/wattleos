# Messages and Billing

The parent portal includes a messaging system for communicating with your child's guides and a billing section for viewing invoices and payment history. These features round out the portal by connecting you to the school community and keeping finances transparent.

## Messages

The messages section provides a direct communication channel between you and your child's guides. Navigate to **Messages** from the parent portal to see your inbox — a list of conversation threads with school staff.

Each thread shows the participants, a preview of the most recent message, and when the last message was sent. Opening a thread displays the full conversation history. You can read messages from guides and reply within the same thread. The messaging experience is the same one that staff use, so there is no difference in how conversations look or function — the only difference is that you access it through the parent portal rather than the staff interface.

Messages are protected by row-level security. You can only see threads where you are a participant. Staff members initiate threads as part of class-level or individual communication, and you can reply within those threads. School announcements and class-wide communications appear in the announcements section rather than as individual messages.

## Announcements

School announcements are broadcast messages from the school to parents. These might include upcoming events, schedule changes, policy updates, or general news. Announcements appear in a dedicated feed and are separate from your personal message threads.

When you view an announcement, it is marked as acknowledged. The school can see which parents have read each announcement, which helps them follow up with families who may have missed important information.

## Billing

The billing section shows your invoices and payment history. Navigate to **Billing** from the parent portal to see a list of invoices associated with your account. Each invoice shows the invoice number, date, total amount, and payment status.

Invoices may include tuition fees, program session charges (from OSHC, extracurricular, or vacation care bookings), late cancellation fees, and any other charges the school has added. Line items are broken out so you can see exactly what each charge relates to.

Payment is processed through Stripe, the payment platform integrated with WattleOS. When you click to pay an invoice, you are taken through Stripe's secure payment flow. Once payment is complete, the invoice status updates to paid and a receipt is available.

If you have questions about a specific charge — for example, a session fee that you believe should have been waived — contact your school's administration. Billing adjustments (waivers, refunds, credits) are made by the school on the admin side and are reflected in your invoice history.

## Re-enrollment

During re-enrollment periods, the parent portal provides access to a pre-filled re-enrollment form. When the school opens a re-enrollment period, you receive a notification (via email or the portal) and can access the form from the Applications section.

The re-enrollment form is pre-populated with your child's current information — name, date of birth, medical conditions, emergency contacts, guardian details, and consent preferences. You review each section, update anything that has changed (a new phone number, an updated medical condition, a new emergency contact), and submit. For most families, re-enrollment is a matter of reviewing and confirming rather than filling in everything from scratch.

After submission, the school reviews your re-enrollment. For families with no changes, the school can batch-approve. For families with updates (new medical information, changed custody arrangements), the school reviews individually. On approval, your child's records are updated in place — nothing is duplicated.

You can also check the status of any enrollment application (new or re-enrollment) from the Applications page, which shows whether it is in draft, submitted, under review, approved, or if changes have been requested.

## What Parents Cannot Access

The parent portal does not include access to administrative functions like managing other families' data, creating announcements, viewing school-wide reports, or accessing the curriculum editor. It also does not show custody restriction details — this is a deliberate safety measure, as in custody disputes both parents may have portal access.

Financial administration (creating invoices, applying waivers, issuing refunds) is handled by the school. You can view your billing information but not modify it.

## Permissions

Messaging, billing, and re-enrollment access are all controlled by your authenticated parent account and guardian relationship. No staff-level permissions are required. If you are logged in and have an active guardian link to a child, all parent portal features are available to you.
